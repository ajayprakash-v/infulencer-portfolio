ABOUT

MADANGOWRI

Indian YouTuber, who mainly uploads videos in the Tamil language. As of February 2022, he has around 6 million subscribers on his self-titled YouTube channel. He is considered one of the most popular South Indian vloggers in India.attended various schools including Siddhu Matriculation School, Madurai, Sainik School, Amaravathinagar, and Dolphin Public School, Madurai. He pursued B. Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Higher Education, Srivilliputhur, Tamil Nadu. He then did an MBA from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. From 2015 to 2017, he pursued a Master of Science (MS), Management, and Business Analytics degree from the State University of New York at Buffalo, United States started his self-titled YouTube channel. In an interview, he shared how he thought of becoming a YouTuber. He said, During my final year of college, I had a tough time as I suffered a break up. As I went into depression, I found solace in YouTube and used it as a tool to redirect my energy. In the initial stages, I didn’t have much of a reach but after a few months, my channel gained momentum.
